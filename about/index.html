About Me.

I’m a third year History student at Durham University. I’m about to graduate and next year I will be studying a Masters in Interactive Journalism at City University. I’m interested in data journalism and specifically interative journalism, Artificial Intelligince and Privacy.

This blog is essentially an extension of my Twitter, where I will expand on ideas related to my interests in more detail as well as being a place to list the projects I’m working on and the things I have done.

This blog is written in RMarkdown and uses Blogdown for static site generation along with a customized version of the Vitae theme for Hugo, all hosted on my Github Pages.
